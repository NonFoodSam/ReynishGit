Our Services

Unrivaled sound mastery

Reynish Audio specialises in crafting the perfect sonic experience for modern metal, metalcore, djent, and pop punk genres. With services encompassing recording, mixing, and mastering, we provide the essential tools to elevate your music to its fullest potential. We're dedicated to refining and enhancing your sound at every stage. From the initial recording sessions to the diligent touches of mastering, trust Reynish Audio to be the driving force behind transforming your musical vision into an extraordinary sonic masterpiece.

Recording

Harnessing 8 years of recording expertise, Reynish Audio cultivates a unique blend of professionalism within a laid-back atmosphere. Expect a recording experience that captures the essence of your music with dynamic precision, ensuring the energetic punch your sound deserves.

Mixing

Your sound is personal, and at Reynish Audio, we understand its importance. Entrust your tracks to us, and experience a meticulous mixing process. You'll have three revisions to ensure your satisfaction, and STEM exports are available upon request. Expect your desired mix with detailed clarity in just a matter of days.

Mastering

Refine your tracks to perfection with Reynish Audio's mastering service. Our approach enhances every detail, ensuring your music achieves its optimal impact and sonic clarity. Available for streaming services, CD, and Vinyl, we ensure mono-compatibility and phase coherence for a seamless listening experience.
